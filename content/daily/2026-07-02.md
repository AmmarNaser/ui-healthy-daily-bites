# Keep Moving for a Healthy Heart

## Short Health Tip  
Regular physical activity is essential for maintaining heart health. Even small amounts of movement can significantly benefit your cardiovascular system.

## Simple Explanation  
1. **Improves circulation**: Regular movement helps your heart pump blood more efficiently, delivering oxygen and nutrients throughout your body.  
2. **Strengthens the heart**: Just like any muscle, your heart gets stronger with exercise, allowing it to work more effectively over time.  
3. **Reduces stress**: Physical activity releases endorphins, which can help lower stress levels, a common risk factor for heart disease.  
4. **Supports weight management**: Staying active helps you maintain a healthy weight, reducing stress on your heart and lowering blood pressure.  
5. **Enhances overall health**: Regular movement reduces the risk of other diseases that can affect your heart, such as diabetes and high cholesterol.

## Quick Tip  
Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate exercise each week, such as brisk walking or cycling, to keep your heart strong and healthy.
