# Prioritize Protein for Stable Blood Sugar

## Short Health Tip
Incorporating protein into your meals can help stabilize blood sugar levels and keep you feeling full longer.

## Simple Explanation
1. **Point 1**: **Protein slows digestion**: Foods rich in protein take longer to break down, which means sugar is released into the bloodstream more slowly.
2. **Point 2**: **Helps maintain energy levels**: Including protein in your diet can provide sustained energy and prevent the peaks and valleys experienced with sugar-only meals.
3. **Point 3**: **Supports muscle health**: Adequate protein intake helps build and repair muscles, which can improve insulin sensitivity and aid in blood sugar management.
4. **Point 4**: **Reduces cravings**: Protein can help you feel fuller for a longer time, reducing the desire for snacks that may spike blood sugar.
5. **Point 5**: **Balances meals**: Pairing protein with carbohydrates can create a balanced meal that minimizes blood sugar spikes.

## Quick Tip
Try adding lean proteins like chicken, fish, or beans to your meals to help maintain stable blood sugar throughout the day.
